Membership

Benefits, tiers, and how to join.

The Museum of Artificial Intelligence is operated by a non-profit association dedicated to educating and enlightening the general public, industry professionals, and academics about the past, present, and potential future of AI. Membership is open to all who wish to collaborate with us in achieving this objective. Membership dues are set at 300 SEK per year for individuals and 5000 SEK for companies.
