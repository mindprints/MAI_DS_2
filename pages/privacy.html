Privacy Policy

The Museum of Artificial Intelligence is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains how we handle personal information.

What we collect

When you submit a membership application or enquiry, we collect your name, email, and any other information you choose to provide.

How we use your data

We use this information solely to process your request, respond to you, and administer memberships. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Retention

Your information is kept only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of collection, after which it will be securely deleted.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at info@aimuseum.se.
