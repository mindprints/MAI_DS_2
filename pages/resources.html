FAQs

Is the museum open to the public?

Currently, visits are by appointment only. Please contact us to book a visit.

What organizations support the museum?

The museum is run on a non-profit basis and is supported by our member association, corporate sponsors and private individuals.

Are your lecturers and workshop facilitators available for hire?

Yes, within the limits of availability. Please contact us to discuss your needs.

What can I expect in a workshop?

A workshop entails a mix of prepatory instructions and hands-on activities. We will help you install the software and tools you need to participate in the workshop. You will work with AI tools and get a good start on using them in your own projects.

What are the qualifications of your lecturers and workshop facilitators?

We are a team of experienced educators and AI researchers. We have a good mix of technical and pedagogical skills and we are all passionate about teaching. We have a significant list of satisfied customers who have praised our workshops and lectures.

What are the prerequisites for attending a workshop?

We cater to both profesionals and individuals who wish to prepare for the future. For the latter categoryno prior knowledge is required, but some basic IT skills are helpful.
