Privacy Policy

The Museum of Artificial Intelligence (“MAI”) respects your privacy. This page briefly explains how we handle personal data when you contact us, apply for membership, or send a booking enquiry.

Controller

Museum of Artificial Intelligence (MAI), Närkesgatan 6, 118 40 Stockholm, Sweden. Contact:info@aimuseum.se

What we collect

Information you provide in our forms, such as your name, email address, organisation and your message or enquiry details.

Purpose and legal basis

  • Respond to enquiries and administer bookings/memberships— performance of a contract and/or legitimate interests.
  • Administration and follow-up(e.g., planning workshops) — legitimate interests.

Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed to handle your request and thereafter only as required for administrative or audit purposes.

Recipients and processors

We useEmailJSto send messages from our forms. They process data as our data processor under their terms. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for their marketing.

Cookies

Our website currently uses only essential cookies. If we add analytics or tracking cookies, we will request your consent before activating them.

Your rights

You may request access, rectification or deletion of your data, and you may object to or request restriction of processing. Contact us atinfo@aimuseum.se. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.
